ONE Jerada Power Plant Monitoring System

Advanced monitoring solution for temperature, gas, humidity, and personnel safety compliance

About ONE Jerada

ONE Jerada is a state-of-the-art power generation facility located in the Jerada region, playing a crucial role in Morocco's energy infrastructure. Our facility combines traditional energy production methods with cutting-edge technology to ensure efficient and reliable power delivery.

With a commitment to safety and innovation, ONE Jerada implements advanced monitoring systems to maintain optimal operational conditions and ensure worker safety at all times.

Monitoring System Features

Environmental Sensors

Real-time monitoring of temperature, gas levels, and humidity throughout the facility to ensure optimal working conditions and equipment safety.

RFID Tracking

Advanced RFID system to track personnel movement and ensure proper authorization in restricted areas of the plant.

Safety Compliance

YOLO-powered video analysis to detect PPE compliance, particularly helmet usage, enhancing worker safety protocols.

Access Monitoring Dashboard

Authorized personnel can log in to view real-time sensor data, RFID tracking information, and safety compliance reports.
